NCEA stands for National Certificate of Educational Achievement. New Zealand Qualifications Auhtority (NZQA) is the agency which oversees NCEA in New Zealand and NCEA was first introduced in 2002. When first starting NCEA and trying to learn more about it can be hard because there's information spread out everywhere, 'NCEA Insider' is made to help breakdown and tell you about what NCEA is in simpler terms and what subject offers there is at Marist College + general information, what assessment procedures are like and there is even a page on study tips and a quick quiz! There is also a page where you can store some of your school information which is easy for you to access when you need it.
