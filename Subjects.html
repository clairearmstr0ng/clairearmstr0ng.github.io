Future Pathways

What are you going to do when you leave school?

It is important to think about what you might want to do as a career to study for when you leave school and many people after high school decide they want to do some form of tertiary education like going to University. To get into courses at University they are dependant on the grades you get from NCEA. Different courses have different requirements. Students who want to study a degree programme at University have to make sure the subjects they study at school can enable them to meet the entry criteria/requirements. Students who do plan on going to University are recommended to take at least four to 5 University Entrance approved subjects.
